Business Development

Whether entering a new market, building a channel partner program, or restructuring revenue — we define go-to-market strategy, identify the right opportunities, and build a sustainable pipeline. Includes competitive mapping, ICP definition, partnership frameworks, and revenue modeling.

Feasibility Studies

Before investing significant capital or time, get a clear-eyed analysis of whether your idea is viable. We conduct market sizing, competitive research, financial modeling, operational scoping, and risk assessment — delivering a decision-ready report.

Grant Research & Applications

From federal SBIR/STTR programs to state economic development grants and foundation funding — we identify opportunities and craft compelling applications, managing the full lifecycle from research through submission and reporting.

Business Process Optimization

Using process mapping, root cause analysis, and lean principles, we identify bottlenecks across your operations. Deliverables include as-is/to-be process maps, improvement roadmaps, and implementation support so changes actually stick.

Data Flow & Transformation

We document and redesign how data moves through your organization — from source systems to reporting. Includes data lineage mapping, transformation logic, tool evaluation, and architecture recommendations for cleaner, more actionable data.

Digital Portfolio Management

Whether managing a project portfolio, investment portfolio, or product suite — we establish governance frameworks, tracking systems, and communication cadences so stakeholders always have the right view of performance and priorities.

Business Strategy

We facilitate strategic planning that produces real clarity. Includes vision and mission development, competitive positioning, SWOT and strategy canvas work, OKR-based goal setting, and 90-day execution roadmaps.

Business Credit & Funding Consulting

Many businesses leave money on the table by not building credit or understanding their capital stack. We guide you through establishing business credit, evaluating debt vs. equity options, SBA programs, CDFIs, revenue-based financing, and investor readiness.

Org Streamlining & Coaching

From one-on-one executive coaching to full organizational redesign — we help leaders and teams operate at their best. Includes org chart analysis, role clarity, leadership development, meeting cadence optimization, and culture alignment.
